The Beginning

Crafted for the Exceptional

Goke Couture was born from a belief that every individual deserves garments that speak their truth. In the heart of Ibadan, we fuse precision tailoring with the rich heritage of African fashion — creating pieces that do not merely fit the body, but amplify the person within.

From the first measurement to the final stitch, every garment passes through hands that have spent years mastering the craft. We work with the finest fabrics, the sharpest patterns, and an attention to detail that borders on obsession.

Our Journey

Milestones in Excellence

2017

Foundation

Goke Couture was established in Ibadan with a small workshop and a big vision: to redefine bespoke tailoring in Nigeria.

2019

First Bridal Collection

Launched our debut bridal collection, garnering attention for its intricate lace work and modern silhouettes.

2021

Expansion

Moved to our current flagship location on Seventh day road, Iyaganku, and expanded our team of craftsmen.

2023

International Reach

Began serving international clients across the UK, USA, and Europe, offering virtual consultations and shipping.

2025

Digital Launch

Launched our fully dynamic website, making the Goke Couture experience accessible to the world.
