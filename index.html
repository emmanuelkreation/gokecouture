From the first measurement to the final stitch, every garment passes through hands that have spent years mastering the craft. We work with the finest fabrics, the sharpest patterns, and an attention to detail that borders on obsession.

The Process

Crafted in
Four Steps

01

Consultation

We begin with a deep conversation about your vision, occasion, preferences, and personality. No detail is too small — this is where your garment is born.

02

Measurement & Design

Precise measurements are taken. A bespoke pattern is drafted — unique to your body, not an adjusted template. Fabrics and designs are selected together.

03

Crafting

Our craftsmen bring the design to life — cutting, stitching, and refining until every element is perfect. Fitting sessions ensure the garment evolves with you.

04

Delivery

Your finished garment is pressed, packaged with care, and delivered on time. The experience does not end at delivery — we stand behind every piece we make.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a bespoke garment take? +
Typically 2–4 weeks depending on the complexity of the garment and the number of fittings required. Bridal gowns and heavily embroidered Agbadas may take 4–6 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline during consultation.
What fabrics do you work with? +
We source the finest fabrics globally — from Italian wool and silk to African Aso-oke, lace, and Ankara. We also work with premium cotton, linen, and blends. Every fabric is hand-selected to match your vision and the occasion.
Do you offer fittings? +
Yes, we offer multiple fitting sessions throughout the crafting process. We believe a garment should be perfected on your body, not just on a mannequin. We schedule fittings at key stages to ensure a flawless fit.
Can I bring my own fabric? +
Absolutely. We are happy to work with fabrics you provide. We will advise on the suitability for your chosen design and ensure the final result is nothing short of exceptional.
Do you cater to international clients? +
Yes. We have worked with clients across the UK, USA, and Europe. We offer virtual consultations, detailed measurement guides, and secure international shipping for your finished garments.
What is your pricing range? +
Pricing varies based on fabric, design complexity, and garment type. We provide a transparent quote after the initial consultation, with no hidden costs. We offer bespoke solutions for every budget.
